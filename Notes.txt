
1. What are regular expressions?
Technique, way to extracting information from text(code,log files, spreadsheets, documents)

2.How do they work?
They match a character with a  specified pattern.
it can match ASCII or unicode characters

Notes on the different expressions types
\d - digits
. - wildcard - match any character except the space
[] - only match a specific character([abc] match only a,b, or c)
